About me

I am an associate professor with the School of Computer Science and Engineering , University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, China. I received the B.Sc. degree in information and computation science and the Ph.D. degree in computer science from the South China University of Technology , Guangzhou, China, in 2009 and 2014, respectively. I visited the Data Mining Laboratory, George Mason University , USA, from 2012 to 2014. My current research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Intelligent Healthcare.
